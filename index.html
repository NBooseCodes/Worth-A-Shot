Overview

Background

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) requires businesses that sell alcoholic beverages to register as a “retail liquor dealer”. In order to keep track of purchases for the TTB, retail liquor dealers need to make sure all of their liquor orders are in one location and able to be accessed when requested to ensure their license to sell alcohol is not revoked. Records should include the type of alcohol received, the date it was purchased, the distributor it was purchased from, and the amount that was received.
